Some faculty Members In The Computer Science Department

Dr Ebenezer Owusu

University of Ghana, Legon, Department of Computer Science
Head of Department
Senior Lecturer

Dr.Ebenezer Owusu is the head of department and a senior lecturer at the University of Ghana, Legon. He joined the Ug Computer Science Department in September 2016 as a lecturer and he is presently the Head of Department for the Computer Science Department. He is a holder of PhD, Msc Bsc Education in Computer Science and he is specialized in Pattern Recognition, Image Processing and Computer Vision.

Dr Michael Agbo Tettey Soli

University of Ghana, Legon, Department of Computer Science
Assistant Lecturer
Senior Software Engineer | PhD Candidate | Co-Founder

Dr Michael is a critical thinker and solutions engineer with over a decade experience in software engineering and algorithm design. With a strong mathematical base, solving complex problems with mathematics is simply another opportunity to exhibit the beauty and elegance of mathematics. His interests are: Graph Theory, Analytic Number Theory, Image Segmentation, Algorithms, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, SDLC, Java, NoSQL, Web Services, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Databases, Financial Markets, Neo4j, Cyber Security, Recommender Systems

    • Dr Mark Atta Mensah

    Assistant Lecturer

    Dr Mark Atta Mensah is a computer science instructor at the University of Ghana, Legon. He holds Master of Philosophy in Computer Science from that same university. Formerly, he was an instructor at BlueCrest University College, a business, technology, and fashion institute. Dr Mensah teaches computing skills using Oracle Academy curriculum. As a graduate teaching assistant in 2015, he became trained in Database, Design and Programming with SQL, and Java Programming. Later he applied those skills at jobs in industry before taking up teaching. In 2019, he was appointed official trainer for Oracle Academy in Ghana.
